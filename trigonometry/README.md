## Key Insights So Far

### Connecting Geometry Proofs to Trig

I realized my perpendicular/path intuition from Kiselev's geometry applies directly to understanding the 45-45-90 triangle:

- **Straight path** M→N = hypotenuse = √2 (shortest)
- **Bent path** M→corner→N = 1+1 = 2 (longer)

This is the triangle inequality in action! The Pythagorean theorem gives us the exact length of the straight path.
